Drayton Recovery Trust reported a net asset value of 139.2p per ordinary
income share as at April 30, against 132.8p at the trust's October year-end
and 110p 12 months earlier.
Attributable revenue for the six months to end-April was flat at Pounds
331,000 for earnings of 4p (4.02p) per share. The interim dividend is
maintained at 4p.
